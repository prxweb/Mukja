Our story

Food is how we say “I care about you”

Within the Korean culture, food is more than just the meal itself — it's a means of displaying a sense of affection and togetherness. The idea of enjoying a meal together is deeply rooted within the language, where 밥 한번 먹자 — “Let's grab a meal” — is used in tandem with “Let's catch up!”

Mukja, meaning 먹자 — “Let's Eat” — was created to share that same sense of togetherness with our loving Montreal community, through authentic Korean dishes we hope you enjoy.

밥 먹었어? “Did you eat?” — a Korean way of asking how you really are.
— Mukja · Montréal
